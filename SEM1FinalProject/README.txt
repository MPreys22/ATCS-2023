The objective of the game is making it to the submit button 3 times without the x axis of the codeblock toaching you.
Essentially even if you are not touching the codeblock is not directly touching you, if you are parallel with it, you lose
Same thing applies with the submit button. You must be above and ahead of the submit button to win 
So if you are above the button and not directly touching it and ahead of its x axis, you still win
